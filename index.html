About

The Porsche 911 GT3 is a high-performance version of the iconic 911, built with a focus on track-ready performance while still being street-legal. It represents the pinnacle of Porsche’s motorsport engineering, known for its naturally aspirated engine, aerodynamic styling, and raw driving experience.

Performance

Powered by a 4.0-liter flat-six engine, the 911 GT3 produces over 500 horsepower and revs up to 9,000 RPM. It accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in just around 3.4 seconds, with precision handling and race-proven suspension systems for the ultimate driving thrill.

Design

Featuring an aggressive aerodynamic body, rear wing, center-exit exhaust, and lightweight materials like carbon fiber, the GT3 is not only built to go fast — it looks the part too. The interior combines a driver-focused layout with motorsport elements like bucket seats and a minimalist dashboard.
